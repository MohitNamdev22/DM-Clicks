ABOUT US

DM Clicks is a Nonprofit Photography Company founded by Dhruv and Moksh in the year 2020 (October, 5). Here we post amazing Nature pictures and reels and also where anyone can submit their Nature Clicks and get featured or mentioned!

But how anyone can submit their Nature Clicks and can get featured or mentioned (if approved) on DM Clicks?? So here are the steps:
1. DM us on any of our Social Media handles which includes Instagram and LinkedIn or you can also submit your Nature Clicks on WhatsApp on the given phone number.
2. You will receive a Confirmation message if your Nature Clicks will be approved.
Ps: You can also submit them on our given Email :)
